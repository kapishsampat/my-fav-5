My favourite hobby

Chess


What is chess?








A game for two people that is played on a board with 64 black and white squares (a chessboard). Each player has 16 pieces which can be moved according to fixed rules across a checkerboard and tries to place the opponent's king in a position from which it cannot escape











Chess is good for your mind and help you improve your strategies. Playing chess results in better brain function, improved memory, cognitive abilities, strategic thinking and attention improvement. It is really fun to play and also helps develope perspective.
